Technical Specifications

  • Polarity: Capitors with polarity (e.g., electrolytic capacitors) and without polarity (Eg:Ceramic,polyster capacitors) are present. For polarised Capacitors like electrolytic capacitors, the terminal side with black strip is negative. Also the one longer lead present will be the positive terminal.
  • Capacitance Value: Measured in Farads (F), it indicates the capacitor's ability to store charge. Common values include 10µF, 100µF, 1mF, etc.
  • Voltage Rating: Indicates the maximum voltage the capacitor can handle without damage. Common ratings are 16V, 25V, 50V, etc.
Note: Always be carefull about the polarity of the electrolytic capacitor.If revered, there are chances for explode.
