Technical Specifications

  • Polarity Check: The longer leg is the Anode (+), and the shorter leg is the Cathode (-). The flat side on the plastic bulb also indicates the Cathode.
  • Forward Voltage (Vf): Typically 2.0V (Red/Yellow/Green) to 3.3V (Blue/White).
  • Max Current: Usually 20mA. Exceeding this will burn out the LED!
Note: Connecting a current-limiting resistor in series with an LED is a good practise. It will prevent it from drawing too much current and burning.
