Technical Specifications

  • Pinout Configuration: For a common NPN transistor like BC547, the pinout is usually Emitter (E), Base (B), Collector (C) when viewed from the flat side with leads pointing downwards.
  • Current Gain (hFE): Indicates how much the transistor amplifies current. For BC547, it typically ranges from 110 to 800.
  • Maximum Collector Current (Ic max): The maximum current the transistor can handle through the collector. For BC547, it's around 100mA.
